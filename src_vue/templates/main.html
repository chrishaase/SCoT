SCoTTi (Semantic Clustering of Tags over Time) is a web application to analyse the topical clusters of hashtags and their evolvement over time.
This is important, for example, for precise information gatherings on hashtags such as #corona.

I have developed the web-app for my Master Thesis in Computer Science/Data Science at the FernUniversität Hagen.
My thesis has been supervised by Dr. Kuepper (A specialist on graphs) and Dr. Keller (A specialist on Software Engineering).

The core of the application is based on the research by Prof. Chris Biemann of the University of Hamburg. (An NLP-Specialist)
SCoTTi is a fork of the open-source software SCoT (Sense Clustering over Time) which is supervised Biemann and co-developed by Haase.
SCoT uses at its core a graph-optimized algorithm “over time” by Biemann, while SCoTTi uses a different algorithm by Haase “in time”.
